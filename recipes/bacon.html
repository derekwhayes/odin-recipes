Bacon!

Description

Bacon created in the oven?!

Ingredients

  • Bacon!

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  2. Arrage bacon(!) in a single layer on prepared sheet with edges
    gently touching or even overlapping.
  3. Bake to desired doneness, 10 to 15 minutes. Remove bacon with tongs,
    and drain on a paper toweled plate.
